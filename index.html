Unveil Friends’ Spotify Activity: Install the Friend Activity Extension

Have you ever wanted to tune into what your friends say on Spotify? The Spotify Friend Activity extension enhances your Spotify Web Player, adding a new layer of social interaction. This smart extension creates a panel that reveals your friends' real-time listening activities, giving you a front-row seat to their musical adventures. However, before you dive in, there are a few things you need to check off the list to enjoy this feature fully. Ensure you're following the Spotify users whose activities you want to track. Your friends must be active on either the Spotify Web Player or the desktop app, having logged in at least once within the past week. Setting Up Spotify Friend Activity: An Easy Installation Guide

Let's start installing the Spotify Friend Activity extension on your device. Moreover, follow these simple steps to begin tracking your friends' music activities easily:

  • Add the Extension to Chrome Kick off the installation by selecting the “Add to Chrome” button.
  • Confirm the Installation Then, click the “Add Extension” button to finalize the installation.
  • Access the Extensions Menu Next, locate and click the “Puzzle” icon on the right end of the Chrome toolbar.
  • Pin the Extension Next, pin the Spotify Friend Activity Chrome extension by selecting the blue pin icon beside its name.
  • Log In to Spotify Next, navigate to open.spotify.com and sign in to your account.
  • Activate the Extension To finish, click on the extension icon. This lets you turn the Friend Activity on or off features according to your preference
